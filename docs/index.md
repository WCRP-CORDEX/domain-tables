# CORDEX Domain Definitions

## Overview

## Domain Definitions

Each of the regions presented is defined by the following parameters:

### A) Parameters needed by an RCM using a rotated pole coordinate system

1. Coordinates of the rotated pole in rotated coordinates:  
   **RotPole (Longitude, Latitude)**

2. Coordinates of the Top Left Corner (TLC) in rotated coordinates:  
   **TLC (Longitude, Latitude)**

3. Number of grid points in the East–West direction:  
   **Nx**

4. Number of grid points in the North–South direction:  
   **Ny**

---

### B) Parameters for an RCM using other coordinate systems (non-rotated coordinates)

5. Coordinates of key domain points in non-rotated coordinates:  
   - TLC (Longitude, Latitude)  
   - CNB (Centre point of Northern Boundary) (Longitude, Latitude)  
   - TRC (Top Right Corner) (Longitude, Latitude)

6. Coordinates of boundary midpoints and domain center:  
   - CEB (Centre point of Eastern Boundary) (Longitude, Latitude)  
   - CPD (Centre point of Domain) (Longitude, Latitude)  
   - CWB (Centre point of Western Boundary) (Longitude, Latitude)

7. Coordinates of lower boundary points in non-rotated coordinates:  
   - BLC (Bottom Left Corner) (Longitude, Latitude)  
   - CSB (Centre point of Southern Boundary) (Longitude, Latitude)  
   - BRC (Bottom Right Corner) (Longitude, Latitude)
